What I Do

Advisor for Latin America of Radisson Hotels, and Royal Hotels. International experience in draft, design and completion of architectural projects, interior design, design of exhibitions and graphic design. Integrated design focused in holistic projects.

Hollistic Approach

Always seeking a plurisensorial interpretation of the environment to offer answers that touch the five senses.

Architecture

Fundamental importance of the physical, social, economical and cultural context. Weighting of spaces and volumes as living containers and aesthetic elements.

Industrial Design

Proposal of elements with character, that contribute to the differentiation of the environment and generate for its originality and excellence in the design a pleasant memory.

Interior Design

Attention for the textures, the colors, the shades of light and shadow, to the maximum detail.

Graphic Design

Amusement without complex to express in a direct and direct way messages and indications.

Interests

Risky and passionate rehearsals in the kitchen Music without borders with wonderful rhythms for listening, humming and dancing.
